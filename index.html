I research, teach, and build software as Systems Faculty in the Software and Societal Systems Department within the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. My research interests lie at the intersection of software engineering, robotics, and program analysis, with a focus on developing and evaluating new languages, tools, and techniques for building, testing, and debugging robotics software.
